To take a test, simply load a CTMT file. The question appears near the top and the answer are in a list in the bottom center. To select an answer, simple click on the answer in the list. Hold control to select multiple answers if applicable. Questions that use a word bank automatically remove words already used.

Use the previous and next buttons to navigate the test. The advanced menu allows you to skip a question or jump to a specific question. At the end of the test, you will have the opportunity to answer any skipped questions and edit any answers.

Once the test has been completed, you have 2 options for saving the results. You can either save the results locally, or upload them using Google Drive. Simply follow the on screen instructions to upload.
